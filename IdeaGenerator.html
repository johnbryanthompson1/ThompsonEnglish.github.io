Idea Generator

Use the resources below to help you get started with a good idea and a clear thesis for your essay.

  • eduwriter.ai

    Allows you to select your subject and type of essay and takes your summary of your general topic before outputting ideas for an essay. It's free but requires signing up.

  • EssayGPT

    Essay GPT uses chatgpt to generate ideas based on the essay type and academic level. It's free but has limits.

  • Claude AI

    Meet one of my favorite LLMs, Claude. Built by Anthropic, Claude is an extremely high peforming generative AI that can help you come up with ideas for your essay.

  • Llama2

    Another great LLM from Meta, Llama2. This playground version of Llama2 can help you come up with ideas for an essay.

  • Essay Topic Generator

    One last idea generator site that I find useful to help you come up with ideas for your essay.
